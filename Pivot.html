Pivot Editor Tool for 3ds Max

This tool streamlines pivot editing in 3ds Max by giving you access to quick presets. Instead of manually adjusting each object's pivot point, you can now simply:

  • Select from common pivot positions such as bottom center, top center, front, back, or center of mass.
  • Apply the chosen preset instantly to one or multiple selected objects.
  • Maintain original transforms and avoid accidental object movement.

Perfect for environment art and asset cleanup workflows where uniform pivot control saves time and reduces errors.
