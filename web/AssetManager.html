Asset Renamer & Replacer for Unreal

This tool is designed to improve asset management in Unreal Engine. You can rename assets quickly, relocate them to a specific folder inside the Outliner, and even replace a selected asset with another from the asset browser or choose one at random from a selection.

  • Batch rename assets using custom rules.
  • Quickly send assets to any folder in the Outliner.
  • Replace current asset with any selected or randomized from a list.
